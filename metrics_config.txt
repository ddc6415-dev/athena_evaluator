======================================================================
ATHENA-1 CRITERIA CONFIGURATION: THE SIX-ACTRESS EVALUATION METRIC
======================================================================

[METRIC_1: ACCURACY]
* Definition: Assess whether the propositional data contains verifiable factual errors, structural deviations, or linguistic noise.
* Target: Ensure high-token linguistic mapping matches grounded reality perfectly.

[METRIC_2: COHESION]
* Definition: Analyze the internal consistency, logical flow, and structural integrity of the text from beginning to end.
* Target: Detect abrupt truncation, missing segments, or broken argumentative links.

[METRIC_3: RELEVANCE]
* Definition: Measure how directly the information addresses the core problem statement or user intent without introducing extraneous filler text.
* Target: Eliminate low-signal prose, hype, and unanchored fluff.

[METRIC_4: GRANULARITY]
* Definition: Evaluate the level of precision, explicit detail, and technical depth present in the data substrate.
* Target: Ensure complex operational structures are broken down into actionable, discrete elements.

[METRIC_5: CLARITY]
* Definition: Examine the visual hierarchy, formatting scannability, and linguistic accessibility of the text block.
* Target: Guarantee instant readability suitable for administrative and analytical documentation.
